Deposits & Payments

Transparent from Day One

💳 How We Handle Deposits

  • Small jobs (under $1,000) — pay on completion, no deposit needed
  • Mid-size jobs ($1,000–$15,000) — 20% deposit to lock in your booking
  • Larger projects — milestone payments tied to agreed stages
  • All deposits receipted immediately via email
  • Scope changes quoted and approved before any additional cost

💰 Payment Options

  • Card — tap-to-pay on-site (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • Bank transfer (EFT) — details on your invoice
  • Payment plans available for larger jobs
  • Invoice sent same day job is completed
  • No surcharges on card payments
